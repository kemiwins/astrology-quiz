Astrology Quiz

CORRECT! Air signs are known for being intellectual, communicative and restless.

NOPE! Gemini, Libra and Aquarius are the air signs. They are known for being intellectual, communicative and restless.

CORRECT! Mercury in retrograde is an ideal time to revisit past projects and to avoid starting new ones.

NOPE! Mercury in retrograde is an excellent time to relax and resume past projects, but not a time for new ventures.

CORRECT! A new moon is an ideal time to plant your wishes for a full moon harvest.

NOPE! The new moon is the best time to make new wishes.

CORRECT! The cardinal signs signal the start of a new season.

NOPE! It's the cardinal signs that are known for taking charge and initiating change.

CORRECT! The ascendent sign is determined by the time you enter the world.

NOPE! The ascendant sign reveals itself during childhood and on first meetings.
